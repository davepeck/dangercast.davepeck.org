Download Mix 007 :: Ein Sof

posted on 07.17.2010 in [ambient abstract infinity]

 

Ein Sof is a Hebrew term that translates roughly as "The Infinite God." The music is ambient, mysterious, euphoric, and just beyond reach. A departure from the electronic contents of previous Dangercast mixes, Ein Sof captures the musical headspace I've inhabited for the past half year.
