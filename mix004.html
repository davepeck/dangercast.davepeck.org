Download Mix 004 :: Kaleidoscopic v2

posted on 02.02.2009 in [rocktronic]

 

In my dreams, I'm dying all the time / then I wake to kaleidoscopic mind.

 

Those Moby lyrics inspired my fourth mix: I wanted music that my mind might hear in its final moments. So I wandered all over the map and came up with this admittedly strange grab-bag of tracks from across the musical spectrum. (As for Moby: bzzzt. You don't make the cut.)
