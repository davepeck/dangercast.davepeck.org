Download Mix 006 :: Nu Summer

posted on 09.23.2009 in [disco 80s revival]

 

Summer of 2009 just came to a close, and everything old is Nu again. Disco is Nu. The 80s? Nu. The occasional crunked-out bit of rocktronica is Nu.

 

The Nu Disco genre encompasses much, but has at its core a strong sense of revival from the past decades. Here's a breezy mix of Nu music, most of which was released in the past few months!
